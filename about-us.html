We Are The Kuilei Beach:

  • Surfers
  • Swimmers
  • Divers
  • Shoreline Harvesters

We Witness the Illegal Camping, and See the deterioration of the environment and safety conditions at the beaches.

We Clean the Beach of liquor bottles, syringes, needles, broken glass, discarded tents, suitcases, and abandoned belongings.

We Surf In, Swim In, Step On, and Smell the human excrement at the Beach

We Send Email to the Mayor, Call HPD, Write Letters to the Media.

We Watch the City Clear out Illegal Campers on One Day and see the Campers return the Next Day.

We Wait for the Monk Seals that Swim near the Shoreline to feel Secure enough to Approach and Nap on the Beach.

We Will Free the Beach!
